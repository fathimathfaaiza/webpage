About Us

Vision :

To be a premier technological institute that moulds qualified and skilled women professionals with creativity,integrity,accountability and environmental concern to excel in their chosen fields and thereby serve to excel in their chosen fields and thereby serve the society.

mission :

  • To empower women through social awareness by offering quality technical education to develop society.
  • To bridge the gap between classroom learning and practical implementation of technology.
  • To help the students find the right career opportunities.
  • To promote a student centered environment.

Courses

Popular Courses

Architecture

The program aims to mould aspiring designers or engineers to become Entrepreneurs in building construction industry and also to assist Architects by giving exposure to various materials, technologies, softwares and services. It also aims to promote the students to pursue higher studies.

Computer Engineering

To mould qualified and skilled women professionals with innovative ideas and creative thinking who can excel in the competitive world of information technology.

Commercial Practice

The Programme aims to mould the students into professionals of international standards in office administration by giving ample exposure to accounting, communication, automation and documentation and also gears up aspiring students to pursue higher education.

Electronics

To serve the society and moulding qualified and skilled women professionals with creativity, integrity, accountability and environmental concern , in tune with advanced technological revolution in Electronics and Communication Engg.
