Womens Polytechnic College Kalamassery

Architecture

The program aims to mould aspiring designers or engineers to become Entrepreneurs in building construction industry and also to assist Architects by giving exposure to various materials, technologies, softwares and services. It also aims to promote the students to pursue higher studies.

mission:

  • Support the students to achieve leadership qualities and excellence in academics by providing better learning environment and quality teaching
  • Enhance students skill in sketching, drafting and designing of built environment.
  • Empower girls to support themselves and their family by building up a career on their on.

Computer Engineering

To mould qualified and skilled women professionals with innovative ideas and creative thinking who can excel in the competitive world of information technology.

mission:

  • To provide a strong theoretical and practical background across the computer science discipline with emphasis on software development.
  • To impart the skills necessary to continue education and grow professional.
  • To inculcate professional behavior, ethical values, team spirit and leadership qualities.

Commercial Practice

The Programme aims to mould the students into professionals of international standards in office administration by giving ample exposure to accounting, communication, automation and documentation and also gears up aspiring students to pursue higher education.

mission:
